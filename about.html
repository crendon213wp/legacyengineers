About Legacy

Legacy Engineers is a full-service mechanical, electrical, plumbing and fire protection (MEP/FP) consulting engineering and construction firm based in Manhattan serving the eastern region of the United States.

The Principals in the firm, John B. Rice and Cheryl McKissack Daniel, bring a combined 60-year track record of success and excellence, to address a need in the market for a strong minority business enterprise with engineering and construction expertise. Legacy provides a full suite of consulting engineering services across all market sectors, including healthcare, education, corporate office, multifamily residential and cultural sectors. Our mission is to create value for our clients as a best-in-class engineering firm and to provide our clients with innovative and sustainable solutions for the built environment. Our goal is to create a diverse Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) firm with a mission to provide access to African-Americans and other underrepresented groups in the engineering field.

Legacy Engineers is comprised of dedicated and experienced engineers with strong capabilities that address the needs of our clients seeking to complete complex projects.

The organization is led by experienced, accomplished professionals, with deep industry knowledge with an attention to detail. We strive to create a work environment that is open, dynamic, empowering to foster an atmosphere of teamwork, positive energy and dedication to our clients needs. Legacy offers a full range of construction and engineering services with best-in-class client service, engineering excellence, innovative skills and a commitment to sustainability that creates long-term value for clients.
