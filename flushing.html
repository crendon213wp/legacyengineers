Client

Flushing Commons

AKF is a proud partner working closely on the Flushing Commons redevelopment project with F&T Group, Rockefeller Group, and Perkins Eastman. AKF is providing a suite of integrated services including MEP/FP engineering, IT/AV and security design and consulting services, lighting design, building controls, and BIM integration for the largest development in Flushing, Queens and one of the largest mixed-use developments in Queens.

Flushing Commons is a region-defining mixed-use development that brings together a talented team of designers, developers, and owners. The multi-phase project includes a full mix of residential, retail, amenity, and community spaces in a seamlessly stitched together element of the greater community. Throughout the design process, the guiding principles were to revitalize the surrounding areas, seamlessly combine live-work-play spaces, and give the massive project an intentional, intelligent, and transformative community experience.

Upon completion of all phases, the project will include:

  • Two commercial office buildings: one 13-story and one 6-story, totaling 216,000 SF
  • Three 17-story residential towers totaling 726,000 SF with 20% marketed to seniors
  • 254,000 SF of retail space including local and national retailers, restaurants, and a multi-screen cinema
  • One acre town square of open space with a fountain plaza
  • Parking for 2,000 cars below grade
  • 53,000 SF community facility featuring amenities such as swimming pool, basketball courts, and exercise room with memberships available to community residents
